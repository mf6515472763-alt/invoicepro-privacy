Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

InvoicePro ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

InvoicePro is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect or transmit personal information to any server unless explicitly stated below.

  • Invoice data (business name, client name, line items, amounts, dates) you enter is stored locally on your device only, and is used solely to generate your invoice preview and PDF.
  • Usage counters (such as how many invoices you've created this month) are stored locally on your device to apply free-plan limits.

2. Data Storage

All invoice data is stored locally in your browser or device storage. We do not upload, sync, or store your invoice content on our servers. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, this information is permanently deleted.

3. Payments

If you upgrade to a paid plan, payment processing is handled by a third-party payment provider (such as Stripe or Paddle). We do not collect or store your payment card details. Please refer to the payment provider's own privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

4. Third-Party Services

InvoicePro does not share your invoice data with third parties. Any analytics or crash-reporting tools used may collect anonymous, non-personal technical data (such as device type or app version) to help us improve the app.

5. Children's Privacy

InvoicePro is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Since your invoice data stays on your device, you are always in control of it. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's local data or uninstalling the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@invoicepro.app
